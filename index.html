Our Story

Crafting Beauty Since 1987

ML
Founding Vision

Born in Paris,
Made for the
World

Maison Lumière began as a single boutique atelier on Paris's Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré, where founder Marie Lefèvre worked alone—sketching, cutting, and stitching each bag by hand. Her philosophy was radical in its simplicity: if a detail would not be noticed, it should not exist. If it would be noticed, it must be perfect.

Over nearly four decades, we have never wavered from that founding commitment. We have grown from a single artisan to a team of 47 master craftspeople, but our process remains unchanged: each bag passes through at least 12 pairs of hands, each bringing a lifetime of specialized expertise.

Today, Maison Lumière is recognized globally as one of the foremost houses of leather goods, yet we remain proudly independent—beholden to our craft, not to shareholders.

Knowledge Base

Frequently
Asked Questions

What leathers do you use?

+
We exclusively use full-grain leathers sourced from family-owned tanneries in Tuscany, Italy. Full-grain is the strongest and most durable part of the hide. Each hide is hand-selected by our sourcing director for quality and natural beauty.

How do I care for my leather bag?

+
We recommend conditioning your leather every 3–6 months using a natural beeswax cream. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight and moisture. For storage, keep your bag stuffed with tissue paper and in its dust bag.

Do you offer bespoke or custom orders?

+
Yes. Our bespoke service allows you to select your leather, colour, lining fabric, hardware finish, and personalised embossing. Bespoke orders require 10–14 weeks and a 50% deposit.

What countries do you ship to?

+
We ship to over 80 countries worldwide via DHL Express. Orders over $500 receive complimentary shipping. All international orders are fully insured and include tracking.

What is your return policy?

+
We offer a 30-day return window from the date of delivery for all unworn, unaltered items in their original packaging. Returns are processed within 5 business days of receipt.

Can you repair older Maison Lumière pieces?

+
Absolutely. We offer lifetime repair services for all authentic Maison Lumière pieces, regardless of age. Services include restitching, hardware replacement, leather conditioning, and re-lining.

How can I track my order?

+
Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a DHL tracking number via email and SMS. You can also log in to your Maison Lumière account to see real-time delivery updates.

Do you offer gift services?

+
Yes. All orders can be gifted with our signature black and gold packaging, a handwritten card, and optional personalised ribbon embossing. Corporate gifting programmes with volume pricing are also available.
